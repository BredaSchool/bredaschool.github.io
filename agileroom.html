Agile Room

Agile was and in some ways still is a countermovement in software development against excessive planning and documentation.
It was also about refocusing the goal towards what value you are actually delivering to the end user.
Somewhere between a bunch of men in Utah deciding to have a conversation and the present day, this message was dilluted, and now we get articles like Is the agile manifesto still a thing? by Atlassian, creators of Jira, Trello & Confluence.

Welcome to the Agile Room! Learn about the history of Agile, what it means for software development, what happened since its inception, and eventually, tackle what it means for creative software development.
